Coca Cola Bottling Aibania celebrate 30 years ...


Tirana, May 20, 2024-Coca-Cola marked the 30th anniversary of the presence in Albania, as brands with strong roots in Albanian lifestyle and as a proud contributor to the country's economic growth. The celebration was held in the Pyramid of Tirana on May 19, on the day on which the production plant began its work for the first time in 1994, honored by the presence of the Prime Minister Mr. Edi Rama, alongside its institutions, clients and partners.

The Pyramid of Tirana symbolizes a bridge between Albania's past and its vision for the future.CCBS, the only official partner manufactures and distributes The Coca-Cola Company products in the market, sees this point as an important space where the journey from the early 1990s to an present development and continuous investment, with a clear vision also in terms of long -term sustainability agenda.

The prime minister also appreciated the event by sharing his artistic interpretation within 30 years of Coca-Cola in the country, which is considered a special link between an international brand like Coca-Cola and Albania.

A memorable toast was raised with Coca-Cola cans created only for this occasion. to celebrate the achievements of the past and with the will to be part of the flourished future of Albania.

At the following institutional moment, the protagonist of the evening in Mother Teresa Square became music, giving all the followers present, the opportunity to cheer with a Coca-Cola talent of Albanian stars, such as Elvana Gjata, Mc Kresha and Lyrical Son.
